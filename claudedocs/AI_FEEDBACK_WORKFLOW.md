## How to Use

### Step 1: Write Your Chapter

Either:
- **Manual Writing**: Type your chapter in the editor
- **AI Generation**: Use "ü§ñ AI Workflow" to generate initial draft

### Step 2: Get AI Reader Feedback

1. Click the **"üí° AI Feedback"** button in the toolbar
2. The AI Feedback panel opens on the right
3. Click **"üé≠ Generate AI Reader Feedback"**
4. Wait ~2-3 minutes while:
   - 6 AI personas read your chapter
   - Each asks 2 thoughtful questions
   - They vote on the most important ones
   - System selects top 3 questions

### Step 3: Review Feedback

You'll see 3 questions like:
- "How does this concept apply to someone just starting out?"
- "What evidence supports this claim?"
- "Could you provide a concrete example of X?"

These reveal what readers found confusing or wanted to know more about.

### Step 4: Improve Chapter

1. Click **"‚ú® Submit Feedback & Improve Chapter"**
2. Wait ~30-60 seconds while AI:
   - Analyzes the questions and discussions
   - Identifies main concerns
   - Rewrites the chapter addressing those concerns
   - Preserves what worked well
3. The improved chapter automatically appears in your editor

### Step 5: Iterate or Publish

**Option A: Iterate Again**
- Review the improved version
- Click "üí° AI Feedback" again
- Get fresh feedback on the new version
- Keep improving until satisfied

**Option B: Publish**
- Happy with the chapter? Click "üì§ Publish Chapter"
- Move to the next chapter
- Repeat the process

## Two Workflow Options

### Option 1: Fully Automated (AI Workflow Panel)

**Best for**: Complete automation from scratch

1. Click "ü§ñ AI Workflow"

4. Click "Analyze Feedback"
5. Choose: "Redraft" or "Keep Current Draft"

**Pros**: Comprehensive, structured process
**Cons**: More steps, longer process

### Option 2: Quick Feedback Loop (AI Feedback Panel)

**Best for**: Fast iteration on existing content

1. Write/have content
2. Click "üí° AI Feedback"
3. Click "Generate AI Reader Feedback"

**Pros**: Fast, simple, 2-click improvement
**Cons**: Less structured, fewer analytics

## Tips for Best Results

### ‚úÖ Do's

- **Start with complete draft** - At least 500 words for meaningful feedback
- **Iterate 1-2 times** - Most chapters improve significantly after first iteration
- **Read the questions** - They reveal real reader concerns
- **Trust the process** - AI improvements often address real issues
- **Use both workflows** - AI Workflow for scratch, AI Feedback for refinement

### ‚ùå Don'ts

- **Don't iterate endlessly** - Diminishing returns after 2-3 iterations
- **Don't ignore all feedback** - If AI readers are confused, real readers will be too
- **Don't skip manual review** - Always read the improved version
- **Don't publish too quickly** - Get at least one round of feedback

## Comparison: AI Workflow vs AI Feedback

| Feature | AI Workflow | AI Feedback |
|---------|-------------|-------------|

| **Steps** | 5-6 steps | 2 steps |
| **Time** | ~6-9 minutes | ~3-4 minutes |
| **Analytics** | Full analysis with scores | No detailed analysis |
| **Best For** | New chapters | Refining drafts |
| **Automation** | Fully structured | Quick & simple |

## Troubleshooting

### "Chapter content is empty"
- **Cause**: No content to analyze
- **Fix**: Write at least 500 words first

### "Failed to generate questions"
- **Cause**: Ollama timeout or connection
- **Fix**: Check Ollama is running, try again

### "Improved chapter seems worse"
- **Cause**: AI misunderstood feedback or overcorrected
- **Fix**: Use Ctrl+Z to undo, try again or edit manually

### Questions seem irrelevant
- **Cause**: Content too short or unclear
- **Fix**: Write more complete draft before getting feedback
