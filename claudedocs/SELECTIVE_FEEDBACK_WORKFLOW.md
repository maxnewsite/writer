## Selection Strategies

### Strategy 1: Address Top Votes
**When**: Most questions have similar concerns
**How**: Select top 3-5 highest voted questions
**Why**: Focus on what most readers care about

### Strategy 2: Address Confusion Points
**When**: Multiple questions reveal misunderstanding
**How**: Select all questions about the confusing concept
**Why**: Fix fundamental clarity issues

### Strategy 3: Add Missing Examples
**When**: Questions ask "how do I..." or "can you show..."
**How**: Select practical/application questions
**Why**: Make content more actionable

### Strategy 4: Balance Perspectives
**When**: Good mix of beginner, skeptic, expert questions
**How**: Select 1-2 from each persona type
**Why**: Make chapter work for all reader levels

### Strategy 5: Skip Irrelevant
**When**: Some questions go off-topic or too narrow
**How**: Only select questions aligned with chapter goal
**Why**: Stay focused on core message

## Tips for Best Results

### ‚úÖ Do's

- **Read all questions first** - Understand full feedback before selecting
- **Select 3-5 questions** - Sweet spot for focused improvement
- **Prioritize high votes** - Popular concerns matter most
- **Look for patterns** - Multiple similar questions = real issue
- **Trust your judgment** - You know your chapter best
- **Iterate deliberately** - One focused pass better than many scattered ones

### ‚ùå Don'ts

- **Don't select everything** - Leads to unfocused rewrite
- **Don't ignore all feedback** - Defeats the purpose
- **Don't select contradictory questions** - AI will struggle to address both
- **Don't rewrite endlessly** - 1-2 iterations usually enough
- **Don't forget to publish** - Perfect is enemy of done

## Troubleshooting

### No Questions Generated
- **Cause**: Chapter too short or Ollama timeout
- **Fix**: Write at least 500 words, ensure Ollama running

### All Questions Seem Irrelevant
- **Cause**: Chapter unclear or AI personas misunderstood
- **Fix**: Revise chapter for clarity first, then get feedback

### Can't Decide Which to Select
- **Cause**: Too many good questions
- **Fix**: Focus on top 3 by votes, or select by category (clarity, examples, evidence)

### Rewrite Doesn't Address Selected Feedback
- **Cause**: Questions too vague or contradictory
- **Fix**: Select clearer, more specific questions

### Button Says "0 Selected"
- **Cause**: Forgot to check boxes
- **Fix**: Click question cards to select them
